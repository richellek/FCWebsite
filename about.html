Why We Exist

The Foster Closet exists to support foster children and the families that care for them. We do this by providing new and gently used clothing, shoes, toys, books, and baby items to these families at no cost.

Our Story

When the Foster Closet began in 2016; it was open twice a month and served 2-5 families each time. Today, it is open every Saturday and serves an average of 10 or more families each week and hopes to do more in the future.
