The Foster Closet accepts donations of clothing (newborn–adult sizes), diapers (2-6), wipes, Pull-ups, new underwear (2T-adult), new socks (infant-adult), shoes (baby-adult), kids and baby items (bottles, furniture, toys), as well as financial contributions. Items can be dropped off at the Foster Closet during business hours or by appointment.

Diapers, wipes, Pull-ups, and new socks/underwear
